About Me

Hi, I’m ELLY OUMA, a passionate photographer based in Kenya. I specialize in capturing real moments — from the calm of the coast to the buzz of the streets, birthday celebrations, and cultural beauty.

With years of experience behind the lens and a strong eye for detail, I bring stories to life through powerful images and videos. My work blends creativity, color, and emotion to create timeless memories.

When I’m not shooting, I’m usually editing, planning the next big session, or exploring new creative styles. Let’s work together and create something unforgettable.

My Services

Here’s what I offer as a professional Photographer and Videographer:

  • Birthday Shoots: Memorable moments from cake to dance floor.
  • Event Coverage: Full photo and video coverage for parties, weddings, and special occasions.
  • Street Photography: Raw, artistic moments captured from daily life in Kenya.
  • Beach & Travel Photography: Stunning visuals from places like Diani and Mombasa.
  • Model Portfolios: Personalized photo sessions for models and influencers.
  • Video Highlights: Edited short reels and highlights for social media or events.
